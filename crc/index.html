Learning

Students at Catholic Regional College Melton experience an exciting and innovative learning journey during their years at the College. Underpinning our work are the core values of instilling a love of learning in our students, a sense of hope and the development of skills and attitudes that nurture strong aspirations in every student. With an expectation of academic excellence always, students are challenged and encouraged to become the best version of themselves. This is achieved through academic endeavours and challenges in every year level.
