Mission:Our mission is to promote education as the fundamental empowerment to success.

NAREN’s goal is to:

1. Advance computer use and broadband access in Nigerian middle schools

2. Promote equal education, especially in the area of providing special care for the underserved so as to thwart inequality in attaining universal education

3. Promote entrepreneurship education through NAREN’s entrepreneurial training curricula and programs

4. Reform school curricula and pedagogy by providing a framework for advancing education through an improved teaching and learning methodology such as:

  • a. Eliminating antiquated teaching methods and improving educators teaching repository
  • b. Implementing a teaching system that seeks to understand student’s learning abilities
  • c. Enforcing accountability on parents and educators and
  • d. Incorporating civic education into school curricula so as to encourage civics knowledge, skills, and dispositions that are critical to the responsibilities of citizenship in Nigeria’s constitutional democracy

OUR PROGRAMS

NAREN has designed several programs that will assist underprivileged students and underserved schools. NAREN programs include:

Entrepreneurship and Innovative Education: This program is designed to create and foster an entrepreneurial ecosystem by training prospective and existing business owners. NAREN has partnered with the Kauffman Foundation, which is the leading foundation of entrepreneurship in the world, to train current and aspiring business owners.

Demand for Digital Education: With books becoming obsolete and the demand for computer use is now a necessity rather than a luxury, NAREN is committed to improving the use of computer in schools in order for students to have free access to information. This program is designed to improve computer access and use through workshops, computer training programs, and supply of computers to selected schools. To make this possible, NAREN has partnered with OLPC to supply computers to selected schools.

Rebuilding Dilapidated Schools: In Nigeria, not only does school lag behind in technology, most public schools need basic schools supplies in classrooms and a better learning environment. Some public schools still lack basic school amenities. NAREN is committed to help create a better learning environment by assisting in rebuilding school facilities.
