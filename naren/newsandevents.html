Educational Provisions of Nigeria Vision 2020

Knowing that education is critical to national development, Nigeria has developed a vision (Vision 2020) and has incorporated educational ingredients in the mix. Vision 2020 is expected to cater for education in a number of ways, some of which include:

  • Curricula review in conjunction with educational policy review with emphasis on skill acquisition, job creation and poverty reduction.
  • Positioning educational programs for global competitiveness, and where necessary, utilizing Public Private Partnership to achieve targets.
  • Provision of ICT capabilities at all possible levels of education.

NAREN looks forward to this and desires to see a country that proceeds to the point of significant transformation, the point where the government recognizes the importance of education and its relation to the growth of the economy... [read more]
