who we are

The National Association for Reforming Education in Nigeria (NAREN) is a nonprofit organization that is committed to rebuild and facilitate a developed educational system by promoting digital literacy, entrepreneurship education and equal access to education. NAREN is committed to empowering youths through education because we believe education is a major factor in eradicating poverty from any economy.

Founded in 2010 and incorporated in New York, NAREN seeks to promote technology, entrepreneurship, and equal access to education.

 

EXECUTIVE STAFF:

Osato Tonia Tongo is a Lawyer, Entrepreneur, Innovator, and a Motivational Speaker. She earned her JD from Florida A&M University College of Law and her BA from John Jay College of Criminal Justice. She has worked for fortune 500 companies and several non-profit organizations. She is also an author of several articles published in the New York Dispute Resolution Journal.

Ms. Tongo is the founder of the National Association for Reforming Education in Nigeria (NAREN). The vision for NAREN was conceived while she was in law school. Ms. Tongo was inspired by the knowledge she gained on education and the laws that govern it. Having being privileged to school both in Nigeria and United States, Ms. Tongo believes that students in Nigeria lag far behind in accessing a quality education compared to students in US. Hence, she is determined to ensure that education is reinstituted as a priority by parents and the government.
