About

Bolt Ventures is a seed and early-stage investment company based in Luxembourg with operating offices in Sao Paulo and Zurich. Founded by European and Brazilian entrepreneurs and investment specialists from several countries, the team has extensive knowledge of building and mentoring start-up businesses but also in seed and angel investments. In July 2013, Bolt Ventures acquired intact Mountain do Brasil corporate and operational structures, including its high growth trajectory businesses of Janamesa, Veduca, Ondalocaland Startupi. The founding partners of Bolt Ventures had previously driven the incorporation, development, operations and investment strategy and execution of Mountain do Brasil.

Investment approach

Key focus of Bolt Ventures is on high-growth internet sectors such as niche e-commerce, mobile, education, marketplaces and subscription-based models. We believe in a hands-on and systematic creation of fast scaling high-growth ventures together with our trusted partners and promote know how & technology transfer of international young high growth companies. We translate proven international business models to local needs for improved time-to-market, attracting the best local talent.
