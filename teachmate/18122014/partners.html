Teach your students at any level

Your success is supported through our strong network of partners and alliances

Boston Children’s Hospital

SpecialNeedsWare has partnered with Boston Children’s Hospital, Harvard Medical School’s renowned teaching hospital, to find solutions for the challenges that individuals with special needs face around the world. In 2014, SpecialNeedsWare began integrating the Visual Immersion System, a revolutionary communication and language methodology developed after decades of research by Dr. Howard Shane, into our technology platforms.

Arc of Nova

SpecialNeedsWare is partnered with The Arc of Northern Virginia on a joint program with the goal of assisting individuals with special needs ride the public transit system independently and provide services during their transition into the workforce. Using our GPS technology, users are provided with appropriate visual supports that are relevant to an individual’s current location. The program has been an overwhelming success and we’re looking forward to working with The Arc of NOVA in the future to expand the program and deepen its impact.

Advisory Board

SpecialNeedsWare has built a diverse and active advisory board consisting of leading educators, researchers and clinical professionals in the special needs community. Our advisors are committed to the success of our partner districts and conduct regular webinars, assist with training and implementation and offer their services to help your students reach their full potential.
