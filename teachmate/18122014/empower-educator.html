Teach your students at any level

Give your educators powerful tools to build diffferentiated instruction

Create

Efficiently create engaging instructional supports such as visual stories, video models, and choice-boards as well as academic lessons of varying levels of difficulty.

Adapt

Modify existing resources with instructional supports to differentiate instruction for students with varying ability levels.

Download

Save time by downloading pre-built content and academic lessons created by educators from around the world on the community content library.

Share

Cloud-based technology allows content and lessons to be shared between educators through out the district, and for members of an IEP team to collaborate effectively to achieve student IEP goals.
