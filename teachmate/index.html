Action Factory

Watch the PuddingStone characters come to life as you navigate through a variety of scenarios targeting who, what and where actions. This exciting game allows you to establish the building blocks of these difficult concepts and work with your learner through identifying each element in isolation. With each selection your learner makes, the game reinforces the concepts using characters to interactively carry out each action. Once your learner is ready to put their knowledge to the test, you can quiz them on each concept in our action factory quiz.

See and do theater

Join Finn and Skyler as they complete a variety of difficult tasks around their home and community. Choose from a variety of locations to target chores, play skills, self-care routines and much much more! Each video provides research based symbols developed by Boston Children’s Hospital to break down the communication interactions that occur in the video allowing your learner to better comprehend what they are seeing. You can control each element of the video to focus the concepts most important to your learner!

Storytorium

Make comprehension of language elements fun for your learner! Choose from a variety of engaging stories to target reading comprehension and literacy skills. Each story offers character based video modeling that can be used to teach difficult concepts that your learner may struggle with.

For those learners who use sign language, each story can optionally be customized with an interpreter allowing you learner to follow along.
