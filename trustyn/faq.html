Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Does signing up with an existing social network have any advantages over creating an account with email? A. Yes, signing up with a social network allows you to recommend people from an existing list of friends and connections and ultimately will save you lot’s of time when creating your network. It’s strongly recommended to sign-up with an existing social network to get the true Trustyn experience.
Q. How is Trustyn different from LinkedIn? A. LinkedIn is built off of self-promotion and the number of connections a person has. Because of this, you can never truly trust the people or the authenticity of anything on LinkedIn. Trustyn on the other hand is built off of people putting their names behind 9 brilliant people of their choice. It’s not about you, but the other person. This creates a practical network of skilled and ethical people.
Q. How does the UI work? A. Trustyn uses an intuitive connection map that allows you to visualize, search, and pin point exactly the type of people you might be looking for on Trustyn. A map over a conventional networking UI also allows for a much more personal and engaging experience.
Q. Do you need to recommend / share 9 people? A. No, in fact you don’t have to share any. Though it’s strongly advised that you add at least 3 people to your network to truly experience and get the most out of Trustyn.
Q. What if I recommend / share somebody that’s not on Trustyn? A. You simply enter in some basic information about the person you’re sharing and that same person will be sent an email prompting them to complete the information. After he or she does this then all the work on your part is complete and the shared person will be given the option to set up an account.
