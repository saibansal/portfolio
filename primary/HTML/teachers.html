Looking for Teachers?

Do you feel that you never have enough time to source and interview potential NQT students?

Do you feel there isn’t enough choice when it comes to taking on a NQT student?

Do you feel that taking on a new NQT student can be costly, especially if that NQT doesn’t work out?

Do you want to select the best match for your school?

Do you want to save time and streamline the recruitment process?

If you have answered ‘yes’ to any of these questions then look no further, we have the solution for you – Primary Connect.

Primary Connect allows you to easily set-up and advertise a position for your school and define the teacher qualities you are looking for. The matching algorithm will use your search criteria and the candidate’s information to then display all candidates that best match the job profile. These candidates will be listed based on their match rating. You can expand a candidate’s profile and view sub-level ratings for each area as well as view their personal statement. There are multiple benefits to you which include:

  • Working smart – 4 step system for selecting and interviewing candidates. Long list and short list for interview, candidate acceptance and scheduling the interview, all at the click of a button.
  • Will save you time and money - information will be collected and analysed for you
  • Provide more choice
  • Provides the best match
  • Maximise your employability
  • Ability to seek out the right NQT
  • Puts you directly in touch with the candidate
  • Ability to store your own ratings and notes following interview and even reject or blacklist an unsuitable candidate
  • Provides a ‘real time’ NQT pool – not limited by local authority borders
  • Helping to connect you to the right people
  • Ability to plan ahead
