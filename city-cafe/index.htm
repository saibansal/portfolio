Who we are

City Caffe is a leading Gurgaon based catering company which delivers delicious food, designed around your needs. Our catering services are the perfect blend of sensational food, inspiring ideas and professional staff. City Caffe entered in the corporate catering in 2010 through the strategic acquisition of catering services. Today City Caffe portfolio is growing and includes some of the country’s most prestigious long-term hospitality and catering contracts.

We employ only those who are passionate and dedicated to creating special guest experiences.
