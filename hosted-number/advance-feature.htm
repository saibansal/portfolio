Advanced Features for your Hosted Numbers

HostedNumbers.com offers several advanced features designed to enhance your hosted
number services. Choosing a custom number for your business and taking advantage of
these built-in services makes it easier to connect with customers, while also enhancing the
professional image for your business.

HostedNumbers.com professional advanced features include:

Call Forwarding

Call forwarding makes it easy to keep in touch with customers when working remotely from home or out in the field. Staying connected has never been easier with Hosted Numbers’ forwarding services, which seamlessly connect one number to another. Whether you have a 800 or a regular local phone number, we make sure that there is zero downtime on your line so you never miss a call. With a fast, easy setup that can be completed in mere minutes, call forwarding from Hosted Numbers is the simplest way of keeping your business running smoothly.

Call Reporting

With HostedNumbers.com call reporting services, you can easily track call count, average call duration and total call duration via reports for every call that you receive. Group data by day, week or month, and display all call data in your preferred layout. Call reporting services from HostedNumbers.com simplifies the tracking of the most important marketing metrics and advertising efforts, including A/B testing and closed loop testing, and is also useful in staffing and customer service tasks.

Call Tracking Analytics Integration

Quickly track call information alongside clicks using any website analytics software. Just set up a blank page on your site and point HostedNumbers.com to that URL in your phone number’s Web Settings. Whenever that number receives a call, HostedNumbers.com will “hit” the chosen URL and collect useful data points that include the caller’s phone number, call start time and duration, phone number dialed and the service ID. With the Quick Wizard for Google Analytics, users can easily use HostedNumbers.com Analytics Integration with the most popular web analytics software available, allowing for quick viewing of call data in GA’s Traffic Sources and Campaigns reports.

Call Recording

Ensure that you are maintaining a professional business environment in all daily communications with call recording services. Perfect for customer service audits, training programs and for ensuring that orders are being entered accurately, call recording services can be enabled to record all calls automatically or can be manually configured at the beginning of each call. Store all calls online in your account for free, for as long as you like. Retrieve them anytime or set up automatic email delivery.

Voicemail

Hosted Numbers offers a full-feature voicemail system complete with caller menu, FindMe, call screening, and email delivery of new messages. Make your business sound more professional and route calls more effectively with Hosted Numbers and our business voice mail options.

Our business voice mail service offers advanced features to help you manage your business communications more effectively and efficiently. These include:

  • Recorded menu prompts
  • Send voicemails to your email
  • Unlimited message storage
  • Caller identification
  • Find-Me, Follow-Me call forwarding
  • Listen online or by phone
  • 24-7 live support

Your number is backed by reliable technology and 24-hour, live customer support from friendly people right here in the United States.

Fax

Hosted Numbers offers a full-feature e-faxing system that allows you to send and receive faxes without a fax machine. Our service turns your ordinary local or toll-free phone number into a powerful communications tool that gives your business a professional image. The same hosted number handles your faxing needs. You don't need a fax machine. You don't need a separate phone number. No more busy signals or lost faxes. Your faxes can be delivered to your email or retrieved online.

Click2Call

Click2Call widgets from HostedNumbers.com can quickly connect site visitors to businesses with an immediate phone call that is placed from the site after a simple click. Users simply enter their phone number and click the “Call Me” button that site owners can easily implement into their existing site code. Once activated, the widget automatically calls the business’s chosen number and then connects them to the visitor’s number. It’s a seamless way to make sure you never miss the opportunity to speak with a potential customer again.

Caller Insight

What do you really know about the customers that are calling you? Caller Insight services from HostedNumbers.com is a powerful solution for getting the demographic information you need about the people that are calling your phone numbers. Caller insight cross-references the caller ID of incoming calls against two of the premium paid demographics databases, giving you instant access to powerful caller stats, including up to 22 critical data points. Review caller data in infographic-style reports to quickly see details that include the caller’s contact information, gender, age, marital status, estimated income and more, all with just a few clicks.
