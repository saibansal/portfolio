About HostedNumbers.com

HostedNumbers.com, the leading provider of hosted numbers for business, is dedicated to helping connect business owners with the hosted phone services they need to keep the business running smoothly.

By utilizing the most powerful technology solutions available for hosted communications, HostedNumbers.com helps businesses connect with customers, no matter where they are located, by offering affordable service packages that have the features that business owners need the most.

At HostedNumbers.com our mission is simple: To provide the best experience and the highest level of customer service to every customer that signs up with us.

Reliable Service for Your Business

As professional communication experts who are dedicated to helping business owners streamline their communications systems, we provide the best possible service experience to ensure that you never have to worry about your system going down.

To maximize uptime, HostedNumbers.com services are backed by our cutting edge data center, which supports:

  • Powerful data redundancy across our vast network of systems
  • A dual-powered network designed with switch and route diversity, including automatic failover on the local loop
  • Disaster recovery backups every 10 minutes
  • Environmental alarms and 24/7 monitoring for emergencies
  • A reliable in-house generator that supplies power up for to 5 days in the event of a power outage

Customer Support You Can Depend On

We provide 24-7 phone support via our expert customer service agents who are conveniently located here, not overseas, in order to offer the most reliable, dependable customer service experience. Before they even take a service call, each of our agents completes a 40-hour training course that includes extensive training on all of our systems, along with hands-on experience in resolving a variety of customer issues.

We hold each of customer reps accountable by performing monthly call audits and by providing continuing training opportunities throughout their career with us. Not only does this ensure that you will reach a highly trained professional when you call HostedNumbers.com, but that your situation will be resolved as efficiently and quickly as possible. Customers can also enjoy fast response times, as we answer 90% of all incoming service calls within 1 minute of coming into our service center.

Customer-Oriented Experience with a Focus on Results

At HostedNumbers.com, we listen to our customers and take all feedback very seriously, especially when refining our services and creating new service offerings. See what HostedNumbers.com can do for your business by calling 800-300-9525 and speaking with a friendly customer service agent today.
