Damascus College

Damascus College is a dynamic Christ-centred learning community, co-governed by the Sisters of Mercy and the Parish Priests of Ballarat. As Ballarat’s only Catholic co-educational secondary college, our innovative and progressive learning inspires and challenges students to become their best and to contribute confidently to the global community.

Damascus College offers a contemporary learning environment, set in a beautiful 50 acre bush setting only 7km from the Ballarat CBD, where our diverse curriculum and vibrant communtity genuinely nurtures respectful relationships that value all members.
