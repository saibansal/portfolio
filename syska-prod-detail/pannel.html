Collapse

The attribute data-parent is used to ensure that all collapsible elements within the defined parent get closed when any one of the collapsible item is displayed.

# Collapsible Group 1

Bootstrap 4 Collapse plugin is used to toogle content to collapse them and open them to show content, thus saving a lot a space and having a lot of content to display within a single page.
