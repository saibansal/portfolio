food

 

“A diet rich in whole plant-based foods have been shown to reduce risks of all cancers.”

---US Institute of Medicine’s report on Exercise, Nutrition, and Cancer Prevention.

The link between diet and cancer prevention can no longer be denied! CP4L supplies you with daily menus and instructional videos to help you prepare meals with whole, plant based foods known for their cancer fighting kick! Making the transition to mainly plant-based eating is the ultimate goal, but you can do this at a pace that is comfortable to you. You are strongly encouraged to try at least two meals per day from our plan, and to maintain at least one meatless day per week.

As you increase the amounts of clean, whole, plant-based food during your day you will find that your body will thank you for it by giving you more energy and a better sense of wellbeing.

 

CP4L will provide you with:

  • recipe bank with over 100 recipes to choose the foods that appeal to you
  • A daily meal plan guide for you to follow if you choose
  • Videos showing how to prepare many of the meals, with added tips for food purhase and storage.
  • Instructions on how to use power foods like quinoa and fresh garlic
  • Guest cooking demos by accomplished vegan chefs
