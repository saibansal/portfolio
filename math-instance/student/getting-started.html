Getting Started

Using MathInstant
It is simple! Just create an account with MathInstant, post your question on the “post question” section on the homepage, and either wait for teachers to reach out to you, or select a teacher from a list of recommendations that suit your math topic. Once you have selected the teacher, you can then schedule for assistance, and have receive math assistance using the interactive whiteboard available on your dashboard.
How do I sign up?
Click on the “ sign up ” button at the top navigation, or click **here** to be redirected to the “ sign up ” tab on MathInstant. You only require your email address and a username. That’s it!
How do I add a question?
There is a search bar on the MathInstant homepage where you can write your question, add relevant files related to the question and edit these files accordingly.
How do I load my balance?
MathInstant supports various payment options for clients. Kindly click **here** to be redirected to the “Payment options” tab for more information. To load your balance, just log in to MathInstant, go to “My Profile” section and add a payment option to your profile.
